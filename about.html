About this blog

My name is Ankit Goyal and I am planning to blog about computer science and engineering in general. My interests span a vast range of topics in CS domain including compilers, OS, competitive programming, parallel algorithms, robotics etc. Previously, I have blogged a bit on Medium about Machine Learning and related topics. In terms of languages, most of my experience is in Python, C++, followed closely by C. I have also worked with Ruby, JS and a bit with Go and C#. I am planning to pick up Rust and some functional programming language next.

Work wise, I am currently employed by Google Taiwan where my work revolves around Pixel graphics pipeline. In the past, I have worked with HackerRank as a TCE (Technical Content Engineer) and later on as SDE (Software Development Engineer). I do not actively work on web development anymore but still very much interested in learning about scalable systems.
